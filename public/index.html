Pest and Disease Detection

Plant Tips:
1. Water your plants early in the morning or late in the evening to minimize water loss due to evaporation.
2. Ensure your plants get at least 6-8 hours of sunlight daily.
3. Use compost to enrich the soil and promote healthy plant growth.
4. Prune dead or diseased leaves to encourage new growth.
5. Rotate your crops to prevent soil depletion and pest build-up.
